About

Dr. Reddy's Foundation (DRF) is a non-profit organization established in 1996 by Dr. K. Anji Reddy, focused on empowering low-income communities in India through improved education, health, and livelihood outcomes, with a particular emphasis on climate action and inclusion, especially for people with disabilities and women. The foundation develops and tests innovative solutions to address social problems, leverages partnerships to scale its impact, and aligns its work with the UN Sustainable Development Goals to create lasting, sustainable change.



Who we are?

Dr. Kallam Anji Reddy is the founder of Dr. Reddy's Laboratories. He founded the company in 1984 with a vision to provide affordable and innovative medicines, and also established the Dr. Reddy's Foundation to support disadvantaged individuals through education and skill development.



What we do

Dr. Kallam Anji Reddy was the scientist, entrepreneur, and philanthropist who founded Dr. Reddy's Laboratories in 1984 to make affordable medicines available globally, playing a key role in the Indian pharmaceutical industry and pioneering drug discovery. He also established the Dr. Reddy's Foundation to provide quality education and skills training to disadvantaged youth, and he received the Padma Shri and Padma Bhushan for his contributions
