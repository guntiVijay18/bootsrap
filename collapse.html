"MSD" is a widely recognized abbreviation for Mahendra Singh Dhoni, a legendary former Indian cricketer and captain. However, "MSD" can also stand for a variety of other things depending on the context, including Musculoskeletal Disorders, a pharmaceutical company (Merck & Co.) operating as MSD outside the United States, the Macromolecular Structure Database, and Moving Standard Deviation in finance, among other terms
